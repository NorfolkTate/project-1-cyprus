This site is dedicated to explaining and understanding the Cyprus Problem, a political and territorial dispute on the island of Cyprus, primarily involving the Greek Cypriot and Turkish Cypriot communities. The conflict originated in the 20th century, leading to a divided Cyprus with the southern part predominantly governed by the Republic of Cyprus and the northern part controlled by the self-proclaimed Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus, recognized only by Turkey.
